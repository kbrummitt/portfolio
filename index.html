About

My approach to security is rooted in a simple principle: you cannot defend what you do not fundamentally understand. For me, that understanding comes from deconstructing systems—exploring their limits, identifying their flaws, and tracing the paths an adversary would take. It’s a curiosity that started with hacking school systems for fun and quickly evolved into a competitive and professional obsession.

This offensive-first mindset is my strategic advantage. It's how I became the youngest Head of Cybersecurity in a county's history just a year after graduating. It's the foundation upon which I architected a proprietary AI-driven threat platform, and it is the core philosophy of my own offensive security firm, Spider Security.
